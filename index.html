M.Sc Computer science - Data science

Unlock the power of data with our MSc Computer Science (Data Science) program at the University of Regina. Dive into the world of data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence in a dynamic and hands-on learning environment. Our comprehensive curriculum, expert faculty, and cutting-edge research opportunities empower you to harness data-driven insights and shape the future of technology. Join a vibrant academic community and embark on a transformative journey towards becoming a data science innovator. Discover your potential at the University of Regina, where data science meets limitless possibilities.


ABOUT PROGRAMS


First Semester (Fall)

CS 700: Software Development Fundamentals
CS 710: Python & Data Fundamentals

Second Semester (Winter)

CS 711: Foundations of Data Science
CS 712: Foundations of Statistics & Machine Learning

Third Semester (Spring/Summer)

CS 713: Applied Machine Learning
CS 714: Big Data Analytics & Cloud Computing



Fourth Semester (Fall)

CS 715: Advanced Data Science & Machine Learning
CS 716: Communication in Data Science

Fifth Semester (Winter)

CS 719: Data Science Seminar & Project
